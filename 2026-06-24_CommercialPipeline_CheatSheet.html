Stage 07
Pedro Brokered
📋 Pedro
Meaning
Brokered carrier placement confirmed. Quote received. Presenting to prospect for binding decision.
Note Required
Carrier · premium · coverage details · presented date
Move to → Waiting to Bind once prospect agrees to move forward
›
Stage 08
Waiting to Bind
⭐ Eva
Gate
Nothing binds until broker fee is paid AND disclosure is signed.
Broker Fee
Confirm broker fee status before moving forward: Agency Bill = invoice + disclosure sent. Financing = disclosure only.
Note Required
Broker fee status · payment method · disclosure sent date
Move to → Bind once fee paid and disclosure signed
›
Stage 09
Bind
⭐ Eva
Meaning
Policy is being bound. Binder issued. All conditions met.
Note Required
Binder number · effective date · carrier · policy number
Binder Delivered
Check Binder Delivered checkbox in AZ when binder is sent to client.
Move to → Awaiting E-Signature for signature completion
›
Stage 10
Awaiting E-Signature
⭐ Eva
Meaning
DocuSign envelope sent. Awaiting client signature to complete binding.
Follow-Up
Follow up with client at 48 hours if unsigned. Flag to Eva at 72 hours if still unsigned.
Note Required
DocuSign sent date · envelope ID · status
Move to → Sold once all signatures complete and policy confirmed
›

Note Required
Policy number · effective date · premium · carrier · bound date
