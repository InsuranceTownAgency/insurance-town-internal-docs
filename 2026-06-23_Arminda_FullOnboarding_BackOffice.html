What Is Smart Cycle
Definition

Smart Cycle is an AgencyZoom feature that pauses a lead and schedules it to return to your queue at a future date. You are not closing the lead. You are parking it to re-engage later when timing is better.

Re-Contact Timing by Lead Type
Lead TypeRe-Contact TimingHow to Calculate
Commercial11 months from lead created dateFind lead created date top right of AZ card. Add 11 months.
Home11 months from lead created dateSame as above.
Bundle (Home and Auto)11 months from lead created dateSame as above.
Auto5 months from lead created dateFind lead created date. Add 5 months.
Note Format After Setting Smart Cycle
Standard format: "Smart Cycled — [reason], re-contact [date you calculated]"

Examples:
"Smart Cycled — no response, re-contact January 14, 2027"
"Smart Cycled — rate too high, re-quote at renewal window — re-contact February 3, 2027"
"Smart Cycled — stale, re-contact March 22, 2027"
Do Not Use Smart Cycle For
  • Leads Eva has specifically told you to close
  • Duplicate dead records (close those, do not Smart Cycle)
  • Any lead outside the authorized pipelines
Tag Rules — Permanent Agency Standard
Hard Rule — No Exceptions

Do not add, change, or remove any tag in AgencyZoom without a specific written instruction from Eva. If you see a missing or incorrect tag, your only action is to note it in your EOD report. Eva decides what gets changed and when.

Bus. Auto Tags

Do not touch Bus. Auto tags at all. Do not flag them. Do not ask about them. They are pending a system clarification. Eva will notify you when this changes.

Correct Tag Format (Reference Only — Do Not Apply Without Eva Instruction)
Two Tag Categories — Agency Wide
  • Primary: [Line] — Main coverage being quoted. Example: Primary: GL, Primary: BOP, Primary: Commercial Auto
  • X-Sell: [Line] — Cross-sell opportunity. Example: X-Sell: Workers Comp

Tags indicate sales opportunities only. Never indicate coverage the customer already has. If you see a tag that does not follow this format, note it in EOD and wait for Eva written instruction.
