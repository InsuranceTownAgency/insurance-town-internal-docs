📊 Monday Board Architecture — Four Groups
John's Monday board has four groups. Status column tracks all stages inside each group. DocuSign Status and KL Acknowledgment Signed are columns inside the groups, not separate groups.
Group 1 — New PL Leads
All new inbound PL leads assigned to John. New contacts from Kat's dialer, walk-ins, referrals, and online inquiries. Work through quote and proposal before moving to next group.
Group 2 — Renewals
All PL policies coming up for renewal. Eva or JoJo will flag renewals. John works the renewal process, re-quotes as needed, and moves through stages until bind or completed.
Group 3 — Servicing and Endorsements
Policy changes, endorsements, address updates, coverage adjustments, and all mid-term service requests. Log every action in AZ notes same day.
Group 4 — Closed
All bound policies, completed renewals, and closed service requests. Move here after Eva confirms completion. Log final AZ note before moving.

🔄 PL New Quote Workflow
1
Receive new lead assignment
New lead assigned from Kat's dialer transfer, AZ pipeline, or Eva Slack message. Create AZ lead record immediately if not already in system.
2
Gather client information
Pull all required info from AZ record. Confirm DOB, address, vehicles or home details, current coverage, prior claims. Fill gaps by flagging Eva, never contacting client directly.
3
Run quote through Farmers first
Always attempt Farmers first for PL. Log quote result in AZ submission note same day.
4
Run brokered quote if Farmers declines or is not competitive
Submit to KraftLake or other brokered PL carrier. Log each attempt in AZ. Name every file: QUOTE_CarrierName_ClientLastName_MMDDYYYY. Upload to AZ File Upload tab.
5
Prepare best quote summary
Identify best quote. Note carrier, premium, and coverage details. Flag Eva for presentation to client. Never present quote to client yourself.
6
Log AZ submission note and flag Eva
Log full AZ submission note. Flag Eva in Slack with: client name, best quote carrier, premium, coverage summary. Eva presents to client.
7
Await Eva confirmation to bind
Client accepts quote. Eva confirms bind in writing via Slack. Never bind without Eva written confirmation.
8
Process DocuSign if required
Send application via DocuSign if carrier requires it. Log envelope in AZ Tab 35. Update DocuSign Status column. Follow up every 3 business days if no response.
9
Send KL Acknowledgment if Mercury or Progressive
If binding Mercury or Progressive through KraftLake, send KL Acknowledgment form before binder is issued. Advance KL Acknowledgment Signed column to Sent. Log in AZ notes.
10
Complete record and move to Closed group
Once binder received and all documents confirmed, log final AZ note. Move Monday board item to Closed group. Include in EOD report with final status.
