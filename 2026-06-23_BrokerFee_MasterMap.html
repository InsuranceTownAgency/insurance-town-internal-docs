Billing Methods

📬 Direct Bill

Carrier bills customer. Collect fee separately via ePayPolicy. Send disclosure via AZ E-Sign.

🏢 Agency Bill

Customer pays agency. Invoice + disclosure sent simultaneously. Email after DocuSign signed.

📑 Financing

Never include fee in financing. Collect separately. Disclosure via AZ E-Sign only.
