Top Priority Hard Rule — Market Submission Order
This rule applies to every single quote from day one. No exceptions ever. Always follow this exact order before going to any outside market.
1
Farmers First — Always
Every commercial quote starts with Farmers. Check Farmers eligibility before anything else. If Farmers can write it, it stays with Farmers. Only move to step 2 if Farmers declines or cannot write the risk.

If Farmers declines: log in AZ notes — Farmers Declined plus reason. Move to KraftLake.
2
KraftLake — Direct Carriers in Dashboard
Go to KraftLake dashboard. Check all direct carriers available for this risk. Submit to the most appropriate carrier based on the risk type and coverage needed. If a KraftLake carrier declines you must log the carrier name, case number, and reason in AZ notes and take a screenshot showing the case ID.

If all KraftLake carriers decline: log each one. Move to step 3.
3
Outside Broker Markets — Only After Steps 1 and 2 Are Exhausted
Only go to outside broker markets after Farmers and all available KraftLake carriers have been attempted and declined. Outside markets include TAPCO, IMACO, US Asia, CID, Connect, and others as applicable. Flag to Eva before submitting to any outside market for the first time on a new risk type.
This order is not optional. It is an agency-wide compliance requirement. Farmers requires that we attempt Farmers and KraftLake before accessing outside markets. Every declination must be documented in AZ notes with case number and screenshot before moving to the next market.
