Broker Fee Process — Agency Bill and Heritage Financing Deals
When does this apply? Any brokerage deal where the billing method is Agency Bill or Heritage Financing requires the full broker fee process below. Direct Bill deals do not require this process. Always check the billing method in AZ before starting.
Never bind before both are confirmed: DocuSign = Signed AND ePayPolicy = Payment Confirmed. For Heritage deals the broker fee must be collected via ePayPolicy BEFORE binding. Heritage finances the premium only — the broker fee is never financed.
