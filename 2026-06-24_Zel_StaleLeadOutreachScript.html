Stale Lead Re-Engagement Scripts

Zel — Commercial Specialist  ·  SMS then Email then Call  ·  June 2026
⚡ Assumable Transfer Rule — Always On No gate. No lock-in. Both yes and no lead to the same outcome: next step or transfer. Create momentum, not pressure.
📌 Re-Engagement Context — Read This First These are NOT cold leads. They already connected with Insurance Town Agency before. They know who we are. Your job is to warmly reconnect, remind them we are here, and find out where they are now. Lead with the relationship, not the pitch.
Channel 1 — SMS First
💬

SMS Re-Engagement

Warm, familiar, one easy question. Send first — wait 24 hours.

Opening SMS
Hey [First Name]! This is Zel with Insurance Town Agency — we worked with you before on coverage for [Business Name]. Just checking in to see where things stand — are you still looking or did you get sorted out?
Follow-Up SMS (no response after 24hrs)
Hi [First Name]! Zel from Insurance Town again 😊 — just wanted to make sure you did not fall through the cracks. We have got some new options that might be a better fit for [Business Name]. Worth a quick look?
Tip: "Did you get sorted out?" — this is a soft open. It acknowledges history without pressure. Either answer (yes or no) gives you something to work with and move forward.
Channel 2 — Email Second
📧

Email Re-Engagement

Send if no SMS response after 24 hours. Personal, brief, one next step.

Subject Line Options (pick one)
Checking back in — [Business Name] We still have options for you, [First Name] Quick follow-up from Insurance Town
Email Body
Hi [First Name], This is Zel from Insurance Town Agency — we connected before regarding commercial coverage for [Business Name] and I wanted to personally reach back out. Things change and we want to make sure you are not leaving coverage on the table or overpaying for what you have. We work with multiple commercial carriers and can usually find a stronger fit quickly. I am going to have our commercial specialist follow up with you directly to take a fresh look. What is the best number to reach you this week? Talk soon, Zel Insurance Town Agency
Key phrase: "I am going to have our specialist follow up with you directly" — assumable. You are not asking. You are telling them what happens next and making it easy to say yes.
Channel 3 — Call Third
📞

Call Script

Warm reconnect. Assumable transfer. No gate ever.

Opening
Hi, is this [First Name]? [Yes:] Hey [First Name]! This is Zel calling from Insurance Town Agency. We had connected before about commercial coverage for [Business Name] — I just wanted to personally reach back out and see where things stand with you. Do you have just 2 minutes?
Reconnect and Qualify
[If still looking / not yet covered:] Perfect timing then. A lot has changed with carrier options lately and I think we can do better for you than last time. Let me ask you a couple quick things so I can connect you with the right person — [If already covered:] That is great to hear! When does your policy renew? We always like to do a quick comparison at renewal to make sure you are getting the best rate — I can have our specialist take a look for you. It only takes a few minutes. [Either way — qualify quickly:] What type of work does [Business Name] do? How many employees do you have? When is your renewal coming up? [Then transfer:] Perfect — I am connecting you right now with our commercial specialist who can pull up some options for you.
Voicemail
Hey [First Name]! This is Zel from Insurance Town Agency — we had connected before about coverage for [Business Name] and I just wanted to personally reach back out. Give me a call at [Phone Number] when you get a chance — I have got some updated options I think will be worth a quick look. Talk soon!
⚡ Assumable Transfer Always: Covered or not covered — both answers move forward. "I am connecting you right now" not "would you like to." Already covered? Renewal review is the next step. Not covered? Fresh quote is the next step. No dead ends.
After All 3 Channels — What To Do

✅ Re-Engaged and Interested

Transfer to Pedro or Eva for quote. Log outcome and transfer in AZ same day. Move stage forward.

→ No Response — All 3 Channels

Smart Cycle in AZ. Log all 3 attempt dates and methods. Done — do not chase further.

⚠️ Duplicate Found

Stop. Flag to Eva in Slack with lead name and what you found. Wait for Eva decision before touching anything.

✗ Hard No or Wrong Number

Log clearly in AZ. Flag to Eva. No further contact.
