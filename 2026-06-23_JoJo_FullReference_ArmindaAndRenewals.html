Three Rules for Working Alongside Arminda
  • If you see a stale or messy record in the early stages leave it alone. Arminda will find it and clean it up. You do not need to touch early stage records.
  • If Arminda ever contacts you directly about a record redirect her to Eva. All of Arminda's questions and flags go to Eva only, not to you. This keeps your work uninterrupted.
  • Never move a record backward into an early stage without telling Eva first. Arminda audits those stages and a backward move could cause confusion.

AZ Note Standard — Renewals and Service Activity
Every action you take on a renewal record must be logged in AZ the same day. Use the correct format below depending on whether this is the first note or an update to an existing one. Never create a new note for the same record — always update the existing one.
New Record — First Time Logging This Renewal
## 📌 Submission Update Carrier: [carrier name] Renewal Date: [date] Current Premium: [amount] Forwarded by Eva on: [today's date] Action Taken: 🟡 Record created and ready to work Next Follow-Up: [Date] at [Time] Handled By: JoJo
Updated Note — Adding Activity to an Existing Record
## 📌 Submission Update — Follow-Up Previously Logged: [Original date] Update Date: [Today's date] Update: 🟢 Carrier 1 — [Carrier Name]: Quoted $[amount] 🟡 Carrier 2 — [Carrier Name]: DocuSign sent — awaiting signature 🔴 Carrier 3 — [Carrier Name]: Declined — [reason] Client Contact: [What was communicated or received] Docs Sent: ✅ Acord ✅ Supplemental App ✅ Loss Runs ✅ Current Policy Dec Next Follow-Up: [Date] at [Time] Status: 🟡 Pending Review / 🟢 Quoted / 🔴 Declined Handled By: JoJo ⚠️ Update this same note each time — never create a new note for the same record.
Log every action same day. If you sent DocuSign today, log it today. If you received a carrier response today, log it today. Never carry logging to the next day.
EOD Standard — Submit Daily by 4:00 PM to #eod-brokerage-jojo
📋 EOD — JoJo | [Date] | [Hours Worked] 💼 QUOTES WORKED: - [Client Name] | [Line of Business] | 🟡 Pending / 🟢 Quoted / 🔴 Declined 🔄 RENEWALS WORKED: - [Client Name] | [Carrier] | [Renewal Date] | [Status: Prepared/Submitted/Proposed/Signed] 📄 DOCUSIGN ACTIVITY: - [Client Name] | [Doc Type] | [Status: Sent/Signed/Expired] 🛠️ SERVICE / CERTs: - [Client Name] | [Request Type] | [Status: Completed/Pending] 🚩 FLAGS FOR EVA: - [Anything you are unsure about, blocked on, or needs Eva review] ⭐ TOMORROW PRIORITY: - [Top 1-3 tasks for your next shift]
EOD must be submitted every day by 4:00 PM even if it was a slow day. Log what you worked on and what is pending. Never skip EOD.

Per Record Checklist — Work Through This on Every Renewal
  • Receive forwarded renewal email from Eva in Slack or email.
  • Create AZ service ticket same day. Confirm record created to Eva in Slack with client name and renewal date.
  • Pull current policy from AZ or Momentum AMP. Note renewal date and current premium.
  • Identify application and supplemental app required by carrier for this line of business.
  • Fill in updated application using prior AZ notes and current business details.
  • Send DocuSign to client with application and supplemental app. Log envelope in AZ Tab 35.
  • Move record to Renewal Packet stage in AZ once DocuSign is sent.
  • Follow up with client every 3 business days if no DocuSign response. Log follow up in AZ notes.
  • Once client signs submit application to correct carrier or GA. Log submission date in AZ notes.
  • Move record to Requote stage after submission.
  • Receive renewal offer from carrier. Prepare renewal proposal for client with final premium.
  • Move record to Propose stage. Send renewal proposal to client via DocuSign. Log in AZ Tab 35.
  • Move record to Waiting Sign/DocuSign stage. Follow up every 3 business days if no response.
  • Once client signs notify Eva in Slack. Wait for Eva written confirmation before binding.
  • After Eva confirms email carrier to bind with signed renewal proposal attached.
  • Once binding confirmation received move record to Completed. Log completion date in AZ notes.
