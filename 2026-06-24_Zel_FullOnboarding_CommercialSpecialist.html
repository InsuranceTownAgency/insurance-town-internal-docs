Three Channel Outreach
💬
SMS First
Send SMS first. Short, friendly, direct. Include business name and your name. Wait 24 hours for response before moving to email.
📧
Email Second
Send follow-up email if no SMS response. More detail, professional tone. Include quote offer and next step. Wait 48 hours.
📞
Call Third
Call if no response to SMS or email. Assume transfer. No gate before transfer. Both yes and no lead to same outcome.
📊
After Outreach — What to Do
✓
Responded and interested: Move lead forward in pipeline. Schedule quote or next step. Log in AZ.
→
No response after all 3 channels: Move to Smart Cycle in AZ. Log note with all 3 attempt dates and methods.
!
Duplicate found: Do NOT touch. Flag to Eva in Slack with lead name and what you found. Wait for Eva decision.
✗
Hard no or wrong number: Log in AZ. Tag as appropriate per Eva instruction. Flag to Eva.
