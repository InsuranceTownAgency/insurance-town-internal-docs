💰 Broker Fee Process
⚡ When Does This Apply?Any brokerage deal with billing method Agency Bill or Heritage Financing requires the full broker fee process. Direct Bill deals do not require it. Always check billing method in AZ before starting.
🚫 Never Bind Before Both Are ConfirmedDocuSign must show Signed AND ePayPolicy must show Payment Confirmed. For Heritage deals the broker fee must be collected via ePayPolicy BEFORE binding. Heritage finances the premium only. The broker fee is never financed.
💵 Fee Bands
Annual PremiumBroker Fee
Under $2,500$200
$2,500 to $4,999$250
$5,000 to $9,999$500
$10,000 to $24,999$750
$25,000 and above$1,000
🔧 9-Step Broker Fee Process
1
Check billing method in AZ
Direct Bill: stop, no broker fee. Agency Bill or Heritage: continue to Step 2.
2
Open Tool 1 from Slack pin
Fill in all fields. Type annual premium. Correct fee band highlights automatically.
3
Select billing method and click ePayPolicy button
Select Agency Bill or Heritage. Heritage warning appears automatically. Click green ePayPolicy button. Create invoice. Copy client payment link before closing.
4
Save form as PDF
Press Ctrl+P or Cmd+P. Select Save as PDF. Name: BrokerFeeDisclosure_[ClientLastName]_[Date].pdf
5
Open Tool 2 — AZ E-Sign Disclosure from Slack pin
Fill all fields. Save as PDF. Upload to AZ lead card Files tab. Send via AZ E-Sign. Log in AZ activity notes same day.
6
Open Tool 3 — Customer Email from Slack pin
Fill all brackets. Paste ePayPolicy link from Step 3. Delete wrong billing block. Send to client from your email.
7
Log to AZ activity notes
Use AZ Submission Note standard. Include LOB, broker fee amount, billing method, AZ E-Sign sent status, ePayPolicy invoice created, next follow-up date. Log same day.
8
Wait for AZ E-Sign Signed AND ePayPolicy Payment Confirmed
Do not advance AZ stage until both confirmed. For Heritage: fee must be paid before binding.
9
Advance AZ to Waiting to Bind and notify Eva
Once both confirmed, advance AZ to Waiting to Bind. Notify Eva in Slack. Wait for Eva written confirmation before binding. Never bind without Eva confirmation.
