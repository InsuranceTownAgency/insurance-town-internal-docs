Insurance Town Agency — Broker Fee Disclosure

Insurance Town Agency charges a broker fee for services rendered in placing commercial insurance coverage on your behalf. This fee is separate from and in addition to the insurance premium charged by the carrier.

Broker Fee: The broker fee for this policy is as indicated above. This fee is non-refundable once the policy is bound.

Payment: The broker fee is due and payable prior to binding of coverage via ePayPolicy. The policy will not be bound until payment is confirmed.

Services Included: Market research and carrier placement, application preparation and submission, policy review and delivery, ongoing service support for the policy term.

By signing below, you acknowledge that you have read, understand, and agree to the broker fee as stated above.
