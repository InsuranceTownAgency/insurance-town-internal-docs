Billing Methods

📬 Direct Bill

Carrier bills customer directly. Collect broker fee separately via ePayPolicy. Send disclosure via AZ E-Sign only.

🏢 Agency Bill

Customer pays agency. Send ePayPolicy invoice AND disclosure simultaneously. Email only after DocuSign signed.

📑 Financing

Never include broker fee in financing. Collect separately via ePayPolicy. Send disclosure via AZ E-Sign only.

Step-by-Step Process
1
Complete the Broker Fee Customer Form
  • Fill all customer and policy fields
  • Enter annual premium — fee band highlights automatically
  • Select billing method — instructions appear below selection
  • Copy the ePayPolicy payment link for Step 3
2
Complete Disclosure and Send via AZ E-Sign
  • Fill all fields on the disclosure form
  • Save as PDF — Ctrl+P → Save as PDF
  • Upload PDF to AZ lead card under Documents
  • Send for E-Sign via AgencyZoom on the lead card
  • Agency Bill only: send ePayPolicy invoice at the same time
3
Send Customer Email with Payment Link
  • Wait until DocuSign disclosure is fully signed
  • Fill in the customer email template
  • Paste the ePayPolicy link into the template
  • Copy and send from your email
4
Log in AZ — Same Day
  • Log broker fee payment received in AZ submission note
  • Confirm disclosure signed and uploaded to lead card
  • Check the binder delivered checkbox on AZ lead card
  • Update lead stage as appropriate
