Broker Fee — VA Flow Guide

Full broker fee process reference  ·  Pedro Torres Jr. and JoJo
Fee Bands
Annual PremiumBroker Fee
Under $2,500$200
$2,500 — $4,999$250
$5,000 — $9,999$500
$10,000 — $24,999$750
$25,000 and above$1,000
Billing Methods

Direct Bill

Carrier bills customer directly. Broker fee collected separately via ePayPolicy. Send disclosure via AZ E-Sign.

Agency Bill

Customer pays the agency. Create ePayPolicy invoice AND send disclosure simultaneously. Send email after DocuSign signed.

Financing

Never include broker fee in financing. Collect broker fee separately via ePayPolicy. Send disclosure only.

Step-by-Step Process
1
Complete the Broker Fee Customer Form
  • Fill all customer and policy fields
  • Enter annual premium — fee band calculates automatically
  • Select billing method
  • Copy the ePayPolicy payment link
2
Complete Disclosure and Send via AZ E-Sign
  • Fill all fields on the disclosure form
  • Save as PDF (Ctrl+P → Save as PDF)
  • Upload PDF to AZ lead card under Documents
  • Send for E-Sign via AgencyZoom on the lead card
  • Agency Bill only: send ePayPolicy invoice at the same time
3
Send Customer Email with Payment Link
  • Wait until DocuSign disclosure is fully signed
  • Fill in the customer email template
  • Paste the ePayPolicy link into the email
  • Send to customer
4
Log in AZ — Same Day
  • Log broker fee payment received in AZ submission note
  • Confirm disclosure signed and uploaded
  • Check binder delivered checkbox on AZ lead card
  • Update stage as appropriate
Standing Rules
You own all 3 steps end to end on your own deals.
Never bind a policy before broker fee payment is confirmed.
Never send the customer email before DocuSign is signed.
Never include broker fee in financing arrangements.
Log everything in AZ the same day action is taken.
ePayPolicy link: https://app.epaypolicy.com/Transactions — log in with your own credentials. Broker fee is always collected upfront before binding.
